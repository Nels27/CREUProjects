Putting a spring back into my step

Coming off my spring break and getting back on track.

Posted by Nelson Raphael March 30th, 2018

It has been a busy past two weeks.

Last week was spring break and the week prior was the week before spring break, so you know every professor was trying to squeeze in another midterm before we left for break.

As I try to get back into the flow of things it is imperative that we integrate the strategies together using the coordinator. Our group member Ali has been hard at work trying to bring all these aspects together and what she requires from us is a high level interface where she can interact with all of the relevant methods for each behavior. So this is my next goal for the next week.

This will bring us closer to our milestone goal, which is to have the snake perform one successful run of the design environment.

I have fallen behind with producing the concept sketches, however, I am going to get back on that so we can reach a decision on those.
